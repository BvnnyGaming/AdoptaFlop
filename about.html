About Us

Who are we?

We are a group of non profit volunteers who bring the world together by taking in homeless rabbits and bunnies with the goal of keeping them well nourished and happy. By offering them for adoption we hope to locate happy families to take care of and love these bunnies and rabbits as we love them.

Our Mission

Our goal is to give homes to as many fluffy bundles of joy as possible! Every bunny deserves to have a kind and loving family to take care of them no whatter what shape or size.
